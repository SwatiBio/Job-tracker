# Statement of purpose

Writes tailored statements of purpose for grad school / fellowship / research programs.

## Options
- tone: Formal|Conversational|Academic|Passionate (Formal)
- length: Short|Medium|Detailed (Medium) — caps 500/1200/2000 chars
- focus: Research|Experience|Goals|Balanced (Balanced)
- includeFuturePlans: bool (yes)

## Structures (in order)
- **Formal** — academic background · research experience · goals · why this program · closing
- **Conversational** — hook · journey · what drives you · why here · vision
- **Academic** — field-context · contributions · methodology · program-fit · research-proposal
- **Passionate** — origin-story · pivotal-moment · growth · commitment · impact-statement

## Steps
1. `waypoint jobs get <id>` — if tied to a specific application
2. read profile (skills, education, experience)
3. pick tone + focus; draft following structure
4. rules: every paragraph ties back to why *this* program · no generic filler · max 2000 chars for detailed · include specific faculty/program mentions if known

## SOP checklist
- [ ] Opens with a clear purpose, not a quote
- [ ] Shows, doesn't tell (specific projects, not "I am passionate")
- [ ] Connects past → present → future arc
- [ ] Names the program and why it fits
- [ ] Closes with intent, not clichés
